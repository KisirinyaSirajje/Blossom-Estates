About Blossom Estates

Your trusted real estate partner in Makerere, Uganda since 2010

Our Story

Blossom Estates was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to help people find their perfect home in the vibrant community of Makerere. What started as a small office with just three agents has now grown into one of the most trusted real estate agencies in Uganda.

Our journey has been defined by our commitment to integrity, personalized service, and deep knowledge of the local real estate market. We understand that buying, selling, or renting property is one of the most significant decisions in a person's life, and we take our role in this process very seriously.

Today, Blossom Estates serves hundreds of clients each year, from students looking for their first apartment near Makerere University to families searching for their forever home and investors seeking profitable opportunities in Uganda's growing real estate market.
